My research lies at the intersection of geometry processing, computer graphics, deep learning, and optimization. I aim to solve computational problems relating to 3D geometry and 3D objects. Currently, I mostly focus on applying techniques from geometry processing in order to devise thoeretically-grounded deep learning approaches for geometric problems.
